# Complex

Complex is a company specializing in aluminum terrace roofs, pergolas, and custom outdoor living solutions. Their current website is outdated, built on WordPress, lacks modern UX, and does not reflect the premium nature of their products. Additionally, managing content requires developer involvement — editors cannot update copy, images, or product info independently.

This project delivers a modern, dark, architecturally inspired website with a headless CMS so the client can manage all content without code. It includes a 3D pergola configurator (color picker for metal elements), an AI garden visualization feature (before/after), and a complete set of lead generation tools.
